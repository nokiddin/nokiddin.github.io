Chat based retail-commerce. Unique model to match local retailers to customers over a chat channel. The "system" consisting of mobile application and a cloud hosted server backend matches customers to local merchants over a chat channel automatically based on location, customer profile and the "fan out" query posted by a customer. The query is sent by the system to matching retailers, who then get to engage with the customer on a chat channel managed by the system. A customer can simultaneously talk to multiple merchants for his shopping query. Merchants can engage customers posting media content for their wares and also converse real time with users.
